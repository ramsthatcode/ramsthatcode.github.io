About Us

Welcome to Rams That Code! We are a group of students passionate about coding and technology.


Rams That Code will host meetings on Tuesdays and Thursdays. We have a discord that will be available whenever for those interested in sharing projects, getting information, or asking questions.


We welcome absolutely everybody, whether you have experience or not. Near the start of the year we will be holding meetings to teach beginners and give them resources.

Events

Rams That Code attends various different types of events, such as hackathons and online programming competitions for those who feel prepared. Along with this, we go to community service events like beach cleanups.
